AYODHYA PROTOCOL
Lanka Reforged
The golden city of Lanka has been seized by the Ravana Protocol — a rogue AI defense network that has reforged the ancient fortress into a war machine. You are an operative of Project Ayodhya, armed with prototype kinetic bows and shockwave tech. Breach the perimeter. Neutralize the sentinels. Shut down Ravana.
Objective
Fight through enemy sentinels patrolling the jungle, then enter the boss arena (northeast of spawn) and defeat the Ravana Protocol — a three-phase boss that grows more dangerous as its health drops.

Combat Tips
Charge your bow longer for up to 3x damage. Dodge rolls give brief invincibility — use them to avoid the boss's AoE slam and projectile barrages. Sprint conserves stamina when you let go, but dodging costs a flat chunk. Fire Arrow deals burn damage over time. Shockwave hits everything in a wide radius — great when surrounded.
Boss — Ravana Protocol
Phase I — Melee strikes and projectile barrages. Keep distance, dodge the patterns.
Phase II (60% HP) — Gains AoE ground slam. Watch for the red flash and dodge out.
Phase III — ENRAGE (25% HP) — All attacks deal 50% more damage and fire faster. Commit to the kill.
