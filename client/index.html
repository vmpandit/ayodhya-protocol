AYODHYA PROTOCOL
Lanka Reforged
In the Treta Yuga, Prince Rama of Ayodhya was exiled by his father's oath, and the demon king Ravana stole his beloved Sita through deception. Armed with celestial Astras from Sage Agastya, allied with the Vanara armies of Sugriv and the devotion of Hanuman, Rama marches on golden Lanka. You walk the path of Dharma — duty to the innocent, devotion to truth, the sacred promise to free Sita from the Ashoka Vatika. Gather allies, speak with sages and warriors, and face Ravana not with hatred but with the resolve that restored Sugriv's throne and earned Jatayu's dying breath. The Amrita in Ravana's navel sustains his ten heads — only the Brahmastra aimed true can end this Adharma. Dharma must prevail.
Objective
Journey through seven chapters of the Ramayana — from the Dandaka forest to the gates of Lanka. Speak with NPCs to learn your chapter goals, gather allies like Hanuman and Angad, and defeat Ravana's forces. The final battle is a three-phase fight against the Demon King himself. Press F to talk to NPCs. Press M to open the map. Press V to meditate.

Combat Tips
Hover your mouse over enemies to target them. Left-click or Q fires a basic arrow. Right-click fires your selected special arrow (cycle with scroll wheel or number keys 1-5). Dodge rolls give brief invincibility — use them to avoid the boss's AoE slam and incoming special arrows. Watch for ⚠ alerts warning of enemy Astras!
Boss — Ravana, the Ten-Headed King
Phase I — The Scholar's Wrath — Ravana attacks with melee strikes and dark Astra barrages. Keep distance, dodge the patterns. His ten heads regenerate endlessly — you must find his weakness.
Phase II — Kailasa's Fury (60% HP) — Ravana channels the power that once lifted Mount Kailasa. AoE ground slams shake the arena. Watch for the red flash and dodge clear.
Phase III — Enrage (25% HP) — Ravana enters a final frenzy. All attacks deal 50% more damage. Remember Vibhishana's secret: the Amrita in his navel sustains his heads. The Brahmastra must strike there.
