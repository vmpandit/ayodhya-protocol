AYODHYA PROTOCOL
Lanka Reforged
The golden city of Lanka has been seized by the Ravana Protocol — a rogue AI defense network that has reforged the ancient fortress into a war machine. You are Lord Rama, prince of Ayodhya, walking the path of Dharma — duty to the innocent, devotion to truth, and the sacred promise to rescue Sita. Gather allies, meditate to find inner strength, and face Ravana not with hatred, but with the righteous resolve that even the gods could not shake. Dharma must prevail.
Objective
Fight through enemy sentinels patrolling the jungle, then enter the boss arena (northeast of spawn) and defeat the Ravana Protocol — a three-phase boss that grows more dangerous as its health drops.

Combat Tips
Hover your mouse over enemies to target them. Left-click or Q fires a basic arrow. Right-click fires your selected special arrow (cycle with scroll wheel or number keys 1-5). Dodge rolls give brief invincibility — use them to avoid the boss's AoE slam and incoming special arrows. Watch for ⚠ alerts warning of enemy Astras!
Boss — Ravana Protocol
Phase I — Melee strikes and projectile barrages. Keep distance, dodge the patterns.
Phase II (60% HP) — Gains AoE ground slam. Watch for the red flash and dodge out.
Phase III — ENRAGE (25% HP) — All attacks deal 50% more damage and fire faster. Commit to the kill.
